Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Tiqan is designed with privacy in mind. Your data is stored securely using Apple technologies, is not used for advertising, and is not used to track you across apps or websites.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Tiqan collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

Tiqan is built to help you train and track your progress while keeping your data private and under your control.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, Tiqan may store:

  • Workout and activity data you enter into the app
  • Health and fitness data, if you choose to grant access through Apple Health
  • Limited, anonymous usage data, such as workouts started or completed and general feature usage
  • Basic technical information required for the app to function properly

The usage data collected is minimal and is not linked to your identity.

How We Use Information

We use information only to provide and improve the app’s features, including:

  • Saving your workouts and progress
  • Displaying workout history, trends, and performance insights
  • Understanding general app usage to improve the experience
  • Improving reliability, stability, and performance

Analytics and Privacy

Tiqan collects limited, anonymous usage data to understand overall app usage and improve features.

  • No personal identifiers are collected for analytics
  • Analytics data is not linked to you as an individual
  • Analytics data is stored securely using Apple CloudKit
  • Analytics data is not used for advertising or tracking

Data Storage

Your data is stored using Apple’s iCloud and CloudKit technologies, which provide secure, encrypted storage.

We do not operate our own servers for storing your personal workout data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Data may only be shared when required by law or when necessary to provide core Apple services, such as iCloud storage.

Health Data

If you choose to connect Apple Health:

  • Health data is used only for the features you enable
  • It is not used for advertising
  • It is not sold or shared with third parties

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide app functionality.

You may remove your data at any time by deleting the app or disabling iCloud access for Tiqan.

Delete Your Data

You can remove your data from Tiqan by deleting the app from your device and disabling iCloud access for the app in your device settings.

If you have granted Apple Health access, you can also manage or remove that access at any time from the Health app or your device settings.

Your Choices

You have control over your data and permissions:

  • You can manage Health permissions in your device settings
  • You can disable iCloud access for Tiqan
  • You can stop using the app at any time

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact support@luna.bh.
